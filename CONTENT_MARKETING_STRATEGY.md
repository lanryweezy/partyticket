## 2. SEO Optimization Strategy

### Keyword Research Focus
- Primary keywords: "events in Nigeria", "party tickets Nigeria", "event booking Nigeria"
- Long-tail keywords: "how to plan a wedding in Nigeria", "campus parties in Lagos"
- Local SEO: City-specific terms like "events in Lagos", "Abuja concert tickets"

### On-Page SEO
- Optimize all pages with relevant keywords
- Use proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3)
- Add alt text to all images
- Implement internal linking between related content
- Ensure fast page load speeds

### Technical SEO
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast loading times (under 3 seconds)
- Clean URL structure
- XML sitemap submission
- Proper use of canonical tags
